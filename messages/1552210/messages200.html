We live in a fast changing world. During the Internet period, we began to spend more and more time alone. But can everyone fully work from home? Some people think yes, others think it's a bad idea.

I believe that absolutely everyone can't work from home. I think it's impossible. First of all, people will always need personal communication, because a person is a social being and without talking, he will simply go crazy. Secondly, many jobs cannot be automated to work from home, and it is very expensive to fully automate all processes.

Many people believe that people will be able to work from home, because everything can be replaced and automated by robots. They do better human work and are cheaper in the long run.

I don't agree with this argument, because if the robots break down, someone will come to fix them, and this is no longer work from home.

In conclusion, I want to say that making everyone work from home is a bad idea. People want to work together on something and feel supported by their colleagues. They are not ready to give up live communication.
