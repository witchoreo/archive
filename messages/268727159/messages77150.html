Let me tell some words about the information that I’ve read.
I know nothing about the author, but I would suppose that he is fond of architecture because he uses such key words as foundation, buildings, sculpture, murals, doors, marble.
The keywords make me think about the subject matter of the information.
The subject-matter of the text is description and historical reference of The Library of Congress.
As for the style of the text it’s considered to be popular.

As for the plot it’s rather simple.
The action takes place in 19-20th centuries in USA.
As for the main character it is President Thomas Gefferson.
So let me tell some words about the facts of the plot of the text.
First of all, the author shows us the main information and purpose of the Nation`s library building.
Also the author mentions how the library`s foundation was laid.
Furthermore, the author epitomizes which buildings the library consists of and how they look like.
In addition, the author says about the decorations of the building – sculptures, materials used and memorials.
So, the main idea is the reference and thorough description of one of the costliest and largest libraries in USA.

In my own conclusion I’d like to develop the main idea.
As for me personally I think that this library represents how National libraries should look like. It is crucial to have libraries like this to spread knowledge among people.
There is such an example in our life as lack of concentration while studying at home or various distractions when learning in the internet, consequently I think that the solution of these problems is building convenient spacious libraries.
As a result of building such luxury library buildings with tons of books to choose people will get more involved in reading, because of the focusing atmosphere of this place.
Even so someone can learn and read at home with the help of the internet but this method has its cons.
After all everyone might realize that learning can be more fun and productive inside of beautifully decorated quiet historical building rather than at home or in front of computer.
Even though we have to meet some inconveniences in order to visit libraries, for example long distance and queues, it is definitely worth it.
Hence it should be necessary to build more beautiful spacious libraries.
Thus it is to be clear that libraries are still needed and have some benefits over the internet and home studying.
